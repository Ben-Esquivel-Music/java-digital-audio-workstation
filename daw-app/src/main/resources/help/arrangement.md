# Arrangement View

The arrangement is a horizontal timeline of tracks containing audio and MIDI
clips. Use the **Edit Tools** to select, trim, split, fade, or draw automation.

## Tips

- Hold **Alt** while dragging a clip to copy it.
- **Ctrl/Cmd + scroll** zooms in and out around the cursor.
- Snap to grid is toggled with **S**.
